Breeds

Big Ol' Hokey Pokey

Species: Gargantua hokeii  |  Origin: Swamp Nebula, Sector 7

He's going to eat a field of flies — and then your entire solar system. His belly glows like a second moon and vibrates at a frequency that makes car alarms go off on three planets.

⚠️ Threat Level: Delightfully Dangerous

The Howlin' Hogger

Species: Ululatus maximus  |  Origin: The Screaming Moons of Zorbax

Listen to the sound of eternal torment — now imagine that sound wearing a tiny cowboy hat. His croak has been classified as a war crime by four intergalactic councils, yet his singing career is absolutely thriving.

🤠 Threat Level: Sonically Catastrophic

Croakin' Gnome Flea Toad

Species: Nanus eructor  |  Origin: Fungal Forests of Planet Mork

These noisy amphibians can be heard for miles — specifically 42.7 miles, as measured by NASA. Scientists believe their belches contain encoded messages. So far the messages just say "feed me" in 14 languages.

📢 Threat Level: Aggressively Pungent
✨ ✨ 👑

Glorp the Magnificent

Species: Glorpius imperialis  |  Origin: Golden Bog of Aurum-9

Arrived on Earth in 1987 inside a meteorite wearing a tiny velvet cape. Glorp has since trademarked the color yellow and charges licensing fees to every daffodil on the planet. His net worth is estimated at 47 billion bog bucks.

💰 Threat Level: Financially Ruinous
💀 💀 ☄️

Squibble McVomit

Species: Ejectus spectacularis  |  Origin: The Cursed Puddles of Urp-6

Don't let the name fool you — Squibble is a distinguished academic with three PhDs. Unfortunately all three are in Competitive Regurgitation. He holds the intergalactic record and is weirdly proud of it at every dinner party.

🎓 Threat Level: Academically Revolting
🔭 🔭 🎓

Professor Blorb Zeetangle III

Species: Intellectus blobicus  |  Origin: The Library Swamps of Cognito Prime

The universe's foremost expert on absolutely nothing useful. His 4,000-page thesis "On the Philosophical Implications of Sitting on a Wet Rock" won the Zorbellian Prize for Literature and also caused three peer reviewers to quit academia forever.

📚 Threat Level: Insufferably Intellectual
